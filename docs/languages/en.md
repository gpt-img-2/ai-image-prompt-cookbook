## How to localize the prompts

- Keep the product, clothing, background, camera angle, and aspect ratio explicit.
- Replace Chinese platform words with your channel: Amazon, Shopify, Etsy, Instagram, Pinterest, RedNote, or marketplace listing.
- If the prompt mentions text on the image, rewrite that text in your own language instead of translating it word by word.
- For brand work, describe the visual direction without asking for protected logos or confusing brand marks.
